Safety

Follow these guidelines to ensure safe operation:

  1. Place smoker on a stable, non-flammable surface, clear of structures.
  2. Keep a 2A:10B:E fire extinguisher nearby; never leave unattended.
  3. Use heat-resistant gloves and closed footwear; keep children/pets away.
  4. Ensure adequate ventilation; avoid use in enclosed spaces.
  5. Allow unit to cool fully before cleaning or moving.

You must adhere to local fire safety and council regulations.
